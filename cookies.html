What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and more useful.

How Wordney Uses Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

  • Session Management: To maintain your session while using our website
  • Security: To protect against fraud and ensure secure access
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences and settings

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services and provide better user experiences.

  • Usage Statistics: To understand which pages are most popular
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix technical issues
  • User Experience: To improve website design and functionality

Extension-Specific Cookies

Our Chrome extension may use local storage (similar to cookies) to store your preferences and settings directly on your device.

  • License Information: To remember your premium status
  • User Preferences: To save your custom settings
  • Recent Definitions: To provide quick access to recent lookups

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

Opting Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. This will prevent Google Analytics from collecting information about your visits.

Extension Settings

You can clear extension data by going to Chrome's extension settings and clicking "Clear Data" for Wordney. This will remove all stored preferences and settings.

Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session while using our website.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and settings for future visits.

Extension Storage

Data stored by the Chrome extension remains on your device until you uninstall the extension or clear the data manually.
