Need Help with Wordney?

We're here to help you get the most out of your vocabulary learning experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install Wordney?
Simply visit the Chrome Web Store, search for "Wordney", and click "Add to Chrome". The extension will be installed automatically and you can start using it immediately.
How do I use Wordney?
After installation, simply highlight any word on any webpage. A definition will appear instantly. You can also click the Wordney icon in your browser toolbar to access settings.
Does Wordney work on all websites?
Yes! Wordney works on virtually all websites including news sites, blogs, social media, and even PDFs viewed in the browser. The extension is designed to be compatible with all web content.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Wordney respects your privacy. We don't track your browsing history, store your reading habits, or collect personal information. All definitions are processed locally when possible.
What's included in the premium version?
Premium users get unlimited definitions, advanced word analysis, pronunciation guides, etymology information, and priority support. The free version includes basic definitions with a daily limit.
How do I update my license key?
Click the Wordney icon in your browser toolbar, go to Settings, and enter your license key in the designated field. Your premium features will be activated immediately.

Troubleshooting Guide

Definitions not appearing
If you're not seeing definitions when highlighting words, try these steps:
  • Refresh the webpage you're on
  • Check if Wordney is enabled in your extensions
  • Try highlighting a different word
  • Restart your browser
Extension not working on specific sites
Some websites may have restrictions that prevent extensions from working:
  • Check if the site allows extensions (some banking sites don't)
  • Try disabling other extensions temporarily
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Contact support if the issue persists
Premium features not working
If your premium features aren't working after entering your license:
  • Verify your license key is correct
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the extension
  • Contact support with your license key
Slow performance
If Wordney seems slow or unresponsive:
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Disable other extensions temporarily
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Restart your browser
